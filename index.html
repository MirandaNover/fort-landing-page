How Fort Works

Fort measures what your muscles do and how they recover, then turns those signals into clear coaching.

  1. 1) Sense

    sEMG shows how hard a muscle is firing. NIRS shows the oxygen available to that muscle. The IMU captures depth and speed.

  2. 2) Understand

    Signals become rep velocity, range of motion, fatigue, and form consistency. You see what changed and why.

  3. 3) Act

    Clear cues during the set and weekly insights help you choose load, volume, and technique with confidence.
