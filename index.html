How Fort Works

Fort turns five signals—temperature, heart rate, motion, muscle activation, and oxygenation—into metrics that support wellness, reduce friction, and show real progress every session.

  1. 1 Sense

    We measure how hard your muscles are working, how much oxygen they have, and how you move, like depth and speed.

  2. 2 Understand

    Signals become rep velocity, range of motion, fatigue, and form consistency. You see what changed and why.

  3. 3 Act

    Real-time insights, daily feedback, weekly trends, and monthly learnings fuel progress. Fort helps you choose load, volume, and technique with confidence.
